Saveetha Teaching Learning Centre


To provide a holistic approach in graduating teacher-centric educators to learner-centric educators to build a brand of students who’ll enjoy learning and establish themselves to constructively contribute in the field of Engineering.


Objectives


  • To become facilitators to encourage self-learning
  • To keep oneself updated to equip the learners
  • To be trained to utilize pedagogical tools
  • Motivated to create self-learners
  • Highly committed to the profession
  • Should be able to encourage learners to become inquisitive, innovative
  • Study and develop customized modules to train the faculty
  • To provide a platform to exhibit their learning

Conference


ICEM’22 is the pioneer edition of Saveetha Teaching Learning Centre (STLC), Saveetha Engineering College (SEC), India. This signature programme is one of the first kinds where the STLC promises to connect the academic experts, budding educators and eminent members of the teaching-learning community. This confluence is all set to explore and enhance the potential of Engineering Educators and Learners. ICEM aims to provide a global platform to identify best practices in teaching and applaud the evolutionary aspects involved in reaching this zenith.

ICEM consists of two key note sessions and paper presentations under two tracks, on 6th May 2022. The research articles submitted to ICEM will be peer-reviewed and selected for prospective publication in Journal of Engineering Education Transformations (JEET) (Scopus Indexed) and few more reputed SCOPUS indexed journals. ICEM’22 look forward to witness the works of passionate educators and learners to be a part of this event.

Guidelines For Submission

Abstract Submission:
Authors are requested to submit the abstract not exceeding 500 words in the following link Click here

Submission of Full-Length paper:
After the intimation of selection, the participants need to prepare a full-length manuscript as per the following guidelines.

  • The preferred length of full paper (including footnotes) is 5,000 words (maximum limit).In general, the authors are suggested to limit the content within 10-12 pages, everything included.
  • The paper must include an Abstract (not more than 150 words) and 3-5 Keywords.
  • Author's autobiographical details should appear as the first footnote of each contribution, and include the name, academic and professional qualifications, institutional affiliation, current title and position of each author.
  • Full paper must be in Times New Roman (normal style and font size 12) and in MS-Word. The page size should be A4, single column with 2.5 cm margin on both sides with single line spacing. All pages of the manuscript (including Tables and Figures) should be numbered.
  • The submissions will be double-blind refereed. After this, the authors will be intimated about the further process of publication in the journal after.

Journals


ICEM 22 publish research articles in prominent journals (SCOPUS INDEXED)


Publishing a Paper in the above-mentioned Journals requires Paper Processing Charges that have to be paid by the submitting author upon acceptance of the Paper for publication following the below criteria.


  • Peer review would be the first process in the selection of a paper which will be evaluated by a group of experts in the same field to make sure it meets the necessary standards for acceptance and publication.
  • Peer review for publication is completely based on the Journal's decision
  • Review occurs during a fixed window of time. All authors are notified of the decision on their paper at the same time after evaluation.
  • Reviewers will give a clear statement of recommodation for each paper and supply comments to support their recommendation suitable for transmission to the author.
  • Authors should agree to comply with the ethical policy of the agreed Journal and understand that publisher has the right to investigate any suspicions and/or allegations of misconduct relating to publications.
