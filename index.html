The submissions need not be restricted only to the above tracks. They may also be pertaining to teaching-learning methods and outcomes.

Guidelines for submission

Abstract Submission: The authors are requested to submit the abstract not exceeding 500 words in the following link (Easy Chair link).

Submission of Full-Length paper: After the intimation of selection, the participants need to prepare a full-length manuscript as per the following guidelines.

  • The preferred length of full paper (including footnotes) is 5,000 words (maximum limit).In general, the authors are suggested to limit the content within 10-12 pages, everything included.
  • The paper must include an Abstract (no more than 150 words) and 3-5 Keywords.
  • Author's autobiographical details should appear as the first footnote of each contribution, and include the name, academic and professional qualifications, institutional affiliation, current title and position of each author.
  • Full paper must be in Times New Roman (normal style and font size 12) and in MS-Word. The page size should be A4, single column with 2.5 cm margin on both sides with single line spacing. All pages of the manuscript (including Tables and Figures) should be numbered.
  • The submissions will be double-blind refereed. After this, the authors will be intimated about the further process of publication in the journal after.
