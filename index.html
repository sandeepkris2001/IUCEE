INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON EDUCATING THE MILLENNIALS (ICEM)'22



ICEM’22 is the pioneer edition of Saveetha Teaching Learning Centre (STLC), Saveetha Engineering College(SEC), India. This signature programme is one of the first kind where the STLC promises to connect the academic experts, budding educators and eminent members of the teaching-learning community. This confluence is all set to explore and enhance the potential of Engineering Educators and Learners. ICEM aims to provide a global platform to identify best practices in teaching and applaud the evolutionary aspects involved in reaching this zenith.

ICEM consists of two key note sessions and paper presentations under two tracks, on 6th May 2022. The research articles submitted to ICEM will be peer-reviewed and selected for prospective publication in Journal of Engineering Education Transformations (JEET) (Scopus Indexed). ICEM’22 look forwards to witness the works of passionate educators and learners to be a part of this event.

Guidelines For Submission

Abstract Submission:
Authors are requested to submit the abstract not exceeding 500 words in the following link (Easy Chair link).

Submission of Full-Length paper:
After the intimation of selection, the participants need to prepare a full-length manuscript as per the following guidelines.

  • The preferred length of full paper (including footnotes) is 5,000 words (maximum limit).In general, the authors are suggested to limit the content within 10-12 pages, everything included.
  • The paper must include an Abstract (no more than 150 words) and 3-5 Keywords.
  • Author's autobiographical details should appear as the first footnote of each contribution, and include the name, academic and professional qualifications, institutional affiliation, current title and position of each author.
  • Full paper must be in Times New Roman (normal style and font size 12) and in MS-Word. The page size should be A4, single column with 2.5 cm margin on both sides with single line spacing. All pages of the manuscript (including Tables and Figures) should be numbered.
  • The submissions will be double-blind refereed. After this, the authors will be intimated about the further process of publication in the journal after.
